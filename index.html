Tharun Saravanakumar

Film, Theatre and digital Production Major

Hello my name is Tharun Saravanakumar and I'm a film, television, theatre and digital production major. Ever since I was a child I dreamed of wanted to be apart of the creation of films. Whether that be acting, directing or writing, I wanted to do it all. To me filmmaking is the greatest art form as it encompasses pictoral, musical, sound, acting and language all togather to make one heck of an expereince.

I've made a feature length crime film called Ruffians. This film was made the summer right after high school and right before freshman year of college. After that I worked on another project in the following summer which was a short film crime story. I followed that up with another short film but this time it was a drama. All of these projects have been leading up the current project I'm working on right now.

The film I'm working on right now has the scale and scope of my first feature film, the sharp and intense nature of my first short film and the dramatic weight of my second short film. The current project I'm working on is called "Far From the Grove" and revolves around the many perspectives and the multifacted nature of the crimanal drug underworld and how that affects innocent people who fall into a life of drug abuse.
